Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 May 2026

🔒 The short version: We collect only what we need to run the app. We never sell your data. We never show you ads. You can delete everything at any time.

1. Who we are

FairwayMate is a golf score tracking app built for golfers who play with their mates. The app is operated by FairwayMate ("we", "us", "our").

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at: hello@fairway-mate.com

2. What data we collect

When you use FairwayMate we collect the following information:

  • Email address — used to create and identify your account
  • Display name — shown to your mates group members
  • Golf handicap — used to calculate Stableford points
  • Profile photo — optional, shown to your group members
  • Golf round scores — the scores you enter during rounds
  • Course information — courses you've played, saved alongside your rounds
  • Mates group membership — which groups you belong to and who else is in them
  • Device type — automatically collected by Firebase to help us improve the app

We do not collect your location, contacts, camera access (except photos you explicitly choose to upload) or any other personal data.

3. How we use your data

We use your data solely to provide the FairwayMate service:

  • To identify your account and keep you signed in
  • To calculate and display your golf scores and Stableford points
  • To show your scores and profile to members of your mates groups
  • To send you a password reset email if you request one
  • To manage your subscription if you upgrade to Premium

We never use your data for advertising, profiling or any purpose other than running the app.

4. Who we share your data with

We share your data with the following third party services that power the app:

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database and file storage. Data stored in London (europe-west2). Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Stripe (Premium subscribers only) — payment processing. We never store your card details. Stripe Privacy Policy
  • Golf Course API — course search data. We send only your search query, no personal data.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with any other third parties.

5. Data storage and security

Your data is stored on Google Firebase servers located in London, United Kingdom (europe-west2).

  • All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS
  • All data is encrypted at rest by Google Firebase
  • Passwords are never stored — handled entirely by Firebase Authentication
  • Access to your data is restricted by security rules — only you and your mates group members can see your data
  • Profile photos are stored in Firebase Storage with access controls

6. How long we keep your data

  • Your account data is kept for as long as you have an account
  • Round history is kept indefinitely so you can track your progress over time
  • If you delete your account, all your data is permanently deleted within 24 hours
  • You can request deletion of specific data at any time by emailing hello@fairway-mate.com

7. Your rights (GDPR)

As a UK or EU resident you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right to access — request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification — correct any inaccurate data (you can do this directly in the app)
  • Right to erasure — delete your account and all associated data (use the "Delete my account" button in the app, or email us)
  • Right to portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — object to processing of your data
  • Right to restrict processing — request we limit how we use your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@fairway-mate.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies and local storage

FairwayMate uses browser local storage (not cookies) to remember:

  • Which mates group you last had active
  • Your course search count for the current month

This data never leaves your device and is not sent to our servers. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser data.

Firebase may set cookies for authentication purposes. These are strictly necessary for the app to function.

9. Children's privacy

FairwayMate is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at hello@fairway-mate.com and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by displaying a notice in the app. The date at the top of this page shows when it was last updated.

11. Contact us

If you have any questions, concerns or requests regarding your privacy or this policy, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@fairway-mate.com
  • Response time: within 30 days

🏌️ FairwayMate is built by golfers, for golfers. We care about your privacy as much as we care about our handicap.
